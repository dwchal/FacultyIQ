This is an R-shiny app that allows upload of an .xlsx file with the following headings:
- Id
- Start time
- Completion time
- Email
- Name
- What is your current academic rank?
- What was the date/ year of your last academic promotion?
- How many peer-reviewed publications do you have in REAIMS?
- What is your Scopus ID?
- What is your Google Scholar username? (highlighted in screenshot below, this includes the letters and numbers between V-J in the search bar)
- Please make note of any specialty associations (e.g IDSA, ACOEM), including regional offices you hold/ have held, national offices you hold/ have held, editorial positions, institutional services (e.g

It is designed to show division-wide metrics. I.e. show graphics of how many publications, citations each person has. Show how this is changing over time. Show how these numbers are associated with academic rank.

The goal is to take a tool that makes it easy to find people who should be put up for academic promotion and to identify deficiencies that people need if they aren't ready for promotion.
